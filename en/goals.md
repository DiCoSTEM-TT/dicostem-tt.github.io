**General objective (GO):** To promote the acquisition of digital competences in education among future primary and secondary teachers through the design and implementation of a metacognitive and multidimensional programme for the development of virtual laboratories that integrate AI and foster effective and meaningful digital STEM learning at different educational levels.

**Specific objectives (SO):**
- **SO1.** Initial diagnosis and analysis of variables in digital, cognitive and emotional domains.
- **SO2.** To develop and design a metacognitive training plan for the acquisition of professional competences (digital, pedagogical and scientific-technological).
- **SO3.** To design and analyse, from a multidimensional perspective, models for integrating virtual laboratories, with and without AI, into STEM teaching and learning at different educational stages.
- **SO4.** To develop digital teaching materials for integration into STEM teaching and learning processes in hybrid environments.
- **SO5.** To design and develop procedures and instruments for student assessment.
- **SO6.** To analyse and evaluate the implementation of digital teaching materials in Virtual Laboratories, with and without AI, for digital STEM Education at different educational levels.
- **SO7.** To disseminate results and conclusions through publications, workshops, seminars and conferences.
