<div class="hero">
  <h1>DiCoSTEM‑TT — Digital Competence and STEM Education in Teacher Training</h1>
  <p class="subtitle">A research project aimed at analysing, designing and validating training proposals for the integration of digital competence, STEM education and artificial intelligence in initial teacher education.</p>
</div>

DiCoSTEM‑TT examines how future primary and secondary teachers can be prepared to integrate digital technology into STEM teaching and learning in a critical and pedagogically grounded way. The project focuses on the design and implementation of virtual laboratories, digital teaching materials and evidence-informed training strategies.

From a metacognitive and multidimensional perspective, the research jointly addresses the cognitive, affective, competence-based and digital dimensions involved in teacher education. It also incorporates the educational use of artificial intelligence as support for pedagogical practice in hybrid and digitalised environments.
